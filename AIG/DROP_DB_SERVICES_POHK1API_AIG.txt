=====================================================================
AIG FOR DROPPING 2 DATABASE SERVICES - POHK1API (ON PROD & DR SERVER)
=====================================================================

Database service names:
api_ro
api_rw

-----
PROD
-----
1. Login HKLPDPS2B001/02/03 standby db servers.

a. Check the Services status on Standby POHK1API DB.

srvctl status service -db POHK1API -s api_ro
srvctl status service -db POHK1API -s api_rw 

b. Check the Services stop on Standby POHK1API DB.

srvctl stop service -db POHK1API -s api_ro
srvctl stop service -db POHK1API -s api_rw 

c. Check if any session through below Services in POHK1API db than kill it before drop.

d. Check the Services remove on Standby POHK1API DB.

srvctl remove service -db POHK1API -s api_ro
srvctl remove service -db POHK1API -s api_rw 


-------------
STANDBY (DR)
-------------
2. Login HKLPDSS2B001/02/03 standby db servers.

a. Check the Services status on Standby POHK1API_DG DB.

srvctl status service -db POHK1API_DG -s api_ro
srvctl status service -db POHK1API_DG -s api_rw 

b. Check the Services stop on Standby POHK1API_DG DB.

srvctl stop service -db POHK1API_DG -s api_ro
srvctl stop service -db POHK1API_DG -s api_rw 

c. Check the Services remove on Standby POHK1API_DG DB.

srvctl remove service -db POHK1API_DG -s api_ro
srvctl remove service -db POHK1API_DG -s api_rw
